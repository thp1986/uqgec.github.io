Column Camellia

Online monitoring desiccation behaviour of Sarmarco Sandy tailings in Column Camellia located on the roof of Hawken Building

Description

The experiment aims to investigate the dessication behaviour of the sandy tailings obtained from Samarco

Parameters monitored includes

--- On the column

8 moisture content

6 temperature

6 soil suction

2 column weight

--- Weather conditions

- temperature and humidity

- barometric pressure

- net infrared radiation

- rainfall

- wind direction and intensity

- ultra violet intensity

- illumination intensity
