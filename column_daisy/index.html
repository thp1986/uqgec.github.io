Description

The experiment aims to investigate the dessication behaviour of the slime obtained from Samarco
Weather and soil conditions are monitored

Parameters monitored includes
---> On the column
-> 8 moisture content
-> 6 temperature
-> 6 soil suction
-> 2 column weight
---> Weather conditions
-> temperature and humidity
-> barometric pressure
-> net infrared radiation
-> rainfall
-> wind direction and intensity
-> ultra violet intensity
-> illumination intensity
