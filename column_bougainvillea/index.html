Column Bougainvillea

Online monitoring desiccation behaviour of Samarco slime tailings in the Column Bougainvillea located at Geo lab first floor

Description

The experiment aims to investigate the dessication behaviour of the slime tailings obtained from Samarco

Monitored parameters

- 9 moisture content

- 8 temperature

- 8 soil suction

- weight loss due to evaporation
